Joseph E. Bohling

Professional Experience

Joe's career includes key positions as Managing Partner and Chief Investor Relations Officer at Pilatus Capital; Managing Director and Member / Owner at Berkeley Research Group, LLC; Co-Founder of The Legacy Academy, a Division of BRG; Co-Founder and President of the Health Transformation Institute, a Subsidiary of BRG; and Chief Human Resources Officer for Aflac, Inc.

Diverse Expertise and Proven Impact

His broad and deep alternative investments, executive, operations, and human capital leadership and consulting experience has spanned numerous industries including e-commerce real estate, supply chain logistics, healthcare, higher education, manufacturing, high tech / clean tech / life science, insurance, financial services, consumer packaged goods, and telecommunications.

Extensive Client Portfolio

His prior assignments and clients have included Hyundai Motor Co., Renault-Nissan-Mitsubishi Alliance, Caterpillar, Vanderbilt University Health, Concentra - Humana, CitiFinancial Auto/Citigroup, PepsiCo/Frito Lay, Comcast Cable, Bridgeport/Yale-New Haven Health System, Parkland Medical Center, and Duke University Medical Center.

Proven Results

Throughout Joe's career, he has consistently delivered for his clients--utilizing a people centric consultative approach--significant cost savings ranging from 10%-30% improvements to the bottom-line and facilitating the process to grow top-line revenue in the range of 105%-200% from prior year performance.

Leadership

Joe is a regular presenter, speaker, and writer for national conferences, periodicals, and news sources expressing his thought leadership on a wide variety of topics ranging from Holistic Prosperity / Wealth Expansion, Intergenerational Wealth Transfer, Executive Coaching / Sustained Peak Performance, Talent Management and Leadership Development, Diversity, Inclusion, Belonging (DIB), Health and Wellness Initiatives, Health Benefits, Change Leadership Methodology, and Enhancing Employee Engagement.

Educational Background

Joe graduated with a Bachelor of Arts Degree from Colorado State University and a Juris Doctor from the University of Colorado School of Law. He has also served as a Board Member and President of the Executive Committee of the Colorado State University Alumni Association Board of Directors.
